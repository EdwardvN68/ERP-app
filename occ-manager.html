OCC Manager – Emergency Response Role

Activation

Activated during any emergency response event requiring EMT coordination involving air crew, aircraft dispatch, or OCC support operations. Activation may be initiated by the Accountable Manager, Director of Operations, or Duty Director of Operations (DOO), as part of the EMT mobilization protocol.

Refer to ERP Section 3.6.9.

Core Responsibilities

  • Advising the EMT on all air crew issues.
  • Liaising regarding services that may be required as well as monitor contract performance with emergency response provider (if applicable).
  • When required, arranging an aircraft to deploy to the emergency/incident site.
  • When required, arranging for air crew to operate any aircraft to the emergency/incident site.
  • As required, appoint a deputy to administer the day-to-day functions of Operations Control and cover and restoring schedule integrity.
  • Providing an accurate list of air crew and position on the aircraft to the EMT.
  • Confirming actual details of cargo and escorts on board.

ERP References

  • Section 3.6.9 – OCC Manager
  • Checklist: No checklist exists for this role in Appendix C.
  • Note: Coordination with OCC Duty Coordinator (Checklist: Appendix B.3) may be expected but is not declared in 3.6.9.

Phase-Based Duties

  • Phase 1 (0–60 min): Confirm crewing issues, air crew availability, and aircraft readiness. Coordinate air movement logistics.
  • Phase 2 (1–4 hrs): Provide updated aircraft and air crew lists, confirm cargo details, and coordinate Operations Control handover if needed.
  • Phase 3 (4+ hrs): Re-establish schedule integrity and shift administration to deputy as required. Monitor air crew needs through EMT.

Cautions

  • Ensure only verified air crew and aircraft details are released to the EMT.
  • Coordinate with OCC Duty Coordinator to avoid duplication of communication or role overlap.
  • Provide continuous updates to EMT if air movements are delayed or revised.
  • Maintain operational oversight until schedule control is fully delegated.
