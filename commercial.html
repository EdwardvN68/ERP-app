Commercial Director (Emergency Management Team Member)

Role Context (Training Overview)

As Commercial Director, you provide client-facing, contractual, and commercial assurance to the Emergency Management Team (EMT). Your role protects organisational relationships, contractual position, and future claims integrity during and after an emergency.

Activation (ERP-Aligned Awareness)

The Commercial Director is engaged when an emergency has actual or potential commercial, contractual, or client-relationship impact and forms part of the EMT as required.

Training note: This page supports role understanding only and does not replace ERP checklists or approved communication processes.

Core Responsibilities (Explained for Training)

  • Act as the EMT focal point for commercial and client-related considerations.
  • Support EMT leadership in understanding contractual exposure and obligations.
  • Coordinate commercial input with Legal, Finance, and Communications functions.
  • Support alignment of client communications with approved messaging.
  • Contribute to protection of ADA’s commercial position during recovery and claims processes.

Role Behaviour Across an Emergency (Training Interpretation)

  • Early phase: Understand potential commercial exposure and client sensitivity.
  • Sustained response: Support controlled, consistent engagement with clients and partners.
  • Stabilisation / recovery: Support claims positioning, contract follow-up, and relationship recovery.

Cautions

  • Do not engage clients or partners outside approved EMT communication pathways.
  • Avoid speculative or unverified information.
  • Coordinate all liability-related matters through Legal and EMT leadership.
