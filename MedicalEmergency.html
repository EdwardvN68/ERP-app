SIMULATION SCENARIO: MEDICAL EMERGENCY

Title: Passenger Cardiac Event – Mid-flight to Fujairah

Date: Saturday, 28 June 2025

Flight: ADA126 – Zayed International Airport to Fujairah (FJR)

Aircraft Type: Dash 8 Q300

Registration: A6-ADB

Crew: 2 pilots, 1 cabin crew

Passengers: 21 (including 1 elderly passenger travelling alone)

Flight Time: 55 minutes

07:34 – IN-FLIGHT MEDICAL EMERGENCY
  • 28 minutes into flight, cabin crew reports:
    "Passenger in seat 3C unresponsive, shallow breathing. Passenger is 72-year-old male. Fellow passenger confirms cardiac history."
  • Cabin crew begins CPR and uses AED onboard
  • Captain alerted and declares PAN-PAN to ATC
ERP Reference: ERP 2.14, ERP 2.3, ERP 2.6.9(b)
07:36 – OCC INFORMED VIA ACARS & ATC
  • OCC Duty Coordinator logs event in Form B.6 (Incident Log)
  • Notifies Duty Director of Operations (DOO)
  • Confirms medical emergency category
  • ATC confirms ADA126 is returning to AUH
ERP Reference: ERP 2.3.1, ERP 2.14, Appendix B.6
07:40 – EMT QUICK RESPONSE STANDBY ALERTED
  • DOO puts EMT Quick Response Team on 1-hour standby
  • OCC coordinates with Airport Medical Services
  • Ambulance confirmed at Stand 12 on arrival
  • Go-Crisis placed on watch in case of fatality notification
ERP Reference: ERP 2.14, ERP 4.7
07:46 – SAFE LANDING & AMBULANCE TRANSFER
  • Dash 8 lands at AUH without incident
  • Paramedics board aircraft immediately
  • Passenger stabilized with oxygen and AED, then evacuated
  • Captain files in-flight medical report per ERP requirements
ERP Reference: ERP 2.14, ERP 2.3.1, ERP 4.7
08:00 – OCC COORDINATES INTERNAL NOTIFICATIONS
  • OCC alerts:
    • Safety Director
    • HR (in case passenger is contractor staff)
    • Communications & Legal (standby)
    • Airline CRM support team assigned to assist fellow travelers
ERP Reference: ERP 3.6.6, ERP 1.14
08:20 – FAC CHECK-IN WITH HOSPITAL
  • Passenger admitted to Sheikh Khalifa Medical Centre
  • Family contact details provided by accompanying friend
  • HR liaises with hospital to ensure family is informed
  • No media action required
ERP Reference: ERP 4.3.5, ERP 4.3.7
10:00 – EVENT REVIEW AND REPORTING
  • OCC compiles event timeline and actions into post-event report
  • Safety logs incident as “Serious Non-Operational”
  • ERP Manual section 2.14 marked for refresher drill in July
  • AED battery replacement flagged in maintenance system
ERP Reference: ERP 1.13, ERP 0.3
