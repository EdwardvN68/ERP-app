Safety Director – Emergency Response Role

Activation

Activated during MEDIUM or HIGH level emergencies when the Emergency Management Team (EMT) is mobilized.

Core Responsibilities

  • Advising the EMT on all emergency management and safety issues.
  • Liaising with law enforcement, regulatory authorities, and security agencies.
  • In consultation with EMT, arranging specialist assistance (e.g., psychology, explosives, interpreters).
  • Confirming through Police sources that any negotiation process with offenders is properly established.
  • Keeping the EMT informed of all authorities’ intentions regarding the emergency.
  • Organizing relief coverage.
  • Notifying the authorities.
  • Acting as the authority to activate Crisis Management Service Provider (Go Crisis).

ERP References

  • Section 3.6.2 – Safety Director
  • Appendix C.6 – Safety Director Checklist
  • Form B.5 – Aircraft Accident/Threat Notification
  • Form SMS-013 – Post-Incident Debrief

Phase-Based Duties

  • Phase 1 (0–60 min): Confirm emergency, notify GCAA, initiate Form B.5, assign EMC liaison
  • Phase 2 (1–4 hrs): Attend EMT briefings, conduct risk assessments, preserve safety data
  • Phase 3 (4+ hrs): Support investigations, submit debrief reports, assist external audits

Cautions

  • Never delay regulatory notification
  • Coordinate closely with OCC and DOO
  • Document all safety actions clearly
