EMT Activation Thresholds

LOW

Routine disruption

Inform and Alert: Routine Management Procedures

Mobilise: Managed by OCC

MEDIUM

Aircraft air or ground incident – NO potential life-changing injuries or fatalities

Inform and Alert: DOO

Mobilise: Quick Response EMT

Missing ADA staff – affecting aircraft operations

Inform and Alert: DOO

Mobilise: Quick Response EMT

Public health issues affecting staff and/or PAX – sufficient scale to affect aircraft operations

Inform and Alert: DOO

Mobilise: Quick Response EMT

Request to conduct SAR operation

Inform and Alert: DOO

Mobilise: Quick Response EMT

Security alert (physical or cyber threat) – NOT specific to ADA aircraft, personnel or facilities

Inform and Alert: DOO

Mobilise: Quick Response EMT

Severe weather forecast, impact within 12 hours – capable of damaging aircraft or preventing flying

Inform and Alert: DOO

Mobilise: Quick Response EMT

Hazardous materials spill – beyond immediate clean-up capabilities

Inform and Alert: DOO

Mobilise: Quick Response EMT

Operating airport/landing spot emergency/disruption – potential no flights for over 12 hours

Inform and Alert: DOO

Mobilise: Quick Response EMT

Damage to aircraft, equipment or facilities affecting operations and exceeding

Inform and Alert: DOO

Mobilise: Quick Response EMT

Media/social media story – ADA not specifically named but potentially financially damaging

Inform and Alert: DOO

Mobilise: Quick Response ERT

HIGH

Aircraft air or ground incident – potential life-changing injuries and fatalities

Inform and Alert: DOO and AM

Mobilise: Full EMT

Fire or explosion at operating facility – threat to life and injury

Inform and Alert: DOO and AM

Mobilise: Full EMT

Specific security threat – ADA named as target of physical or cyber-attack (to ADA staff, equipment, or facilities)

Inform and Alert: DOO and AM

Mobilise: Full EMT

AM – Accountable Manager

DOO – Duty Director of Operations
