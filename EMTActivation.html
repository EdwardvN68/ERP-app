EMT Activation Matrix

Incident Type Inform and Alert Mobilise
LOW
Routine disruption Routine Management Procedures Managed by OCC
MEDIUM
Aircraft air or ground incident – NO potential life changing injuries or fatalities DOO Quick Response EMT
Missing ADA staff – affecting aircraft operations DOO Quick Response EMT
Public health issues affecting staff and/or PAX – sufficient scale to affect aircraft operations DOO Quick Response EMT
Request to conduct SAR operation DOO Quick Response EMT
Security alert (physical or cyber threat) – NOT specific to ADA aircraft, personnel or facilities DOO Quick Response EMT
Severe weather forecast, within 12 hours – capable of damaging aircraft or preventing flying DOO Quick Response EMT
Hazardous materials spill – beyond immediate clean-up capabilities DOO Quick Response EMT
Operating airport/landing spot emergency/disruption with potential no flights for over 12 hours DOO Quick Response EMT
Damage to aircraft, equipment or facilities affecting operations and exceeding limits DOO Quick Response EMT
Media/social media story, ADA not specifically named, potentially financially damaging DOO Quick Response ERT
HIGH
Aircraft air or ground incident – potential life changing injuries and fatalities DOO and AM Full EMT
Fire or explosion at operating facility – threat to life and/or injury DOO and AM Full EMT
Specific security threat – ADA named as target of physical or cyber-attack to ADA staff, equipment or facilities DOO and AM Full EMT

•  Accountable Manager (AM)
