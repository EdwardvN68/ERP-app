Other Members – Emergency Management Centre Role

Activation

Activated selectively during full EMT mobilizations where additional departmental or subject matter expertise is required. These members are drawn from relevant departments and are called to support the EMT as needed.

Refer to ERP Sections 2.5.1, 2.5.2, 3.3.3, 3.6.14 and Appendix C.16.

Core Responsibilities

  • Support the EMT by providing expertise relevant to their departmental functions.
  • Attend EMT meetings if requested and carry out assigned emergency-related duties.
  • Coordinate between their home department and the EMT for status updates and support.
  • Ensure continuity and response readiness in their department during emergencies.
  • Follow EMT directions and report actions, observations, and constraints promptly.

ERP References

  • Section 2.5.1 – EMT Activation Thresholds
  • Section 2.5.2 – EMT On-Call Roles
  • Section 3.3.3 – EMT On-Call Roles

  • Appendix C.16 – EMT General Checklist

Phase-Based Duties

  • Phase 1 (0–60 min): Remain on standby; prepare for EMT call-in; ensure departmental continuity readiness.
  • Phase 2 (1–4 hrs): Join EMT if requested; assist with operational status reports and inter-departmental actions.
  • Phase 3 (4+ hrs): Continue support operations; assist with transition to recovery and post-incident reporting.

Cautions

  • Do not self-deploy to the EMC without official instruction.
  • Refrain from giving public or media statements unless authorized.
  • Ensure departmental logs are properly documented and secured.
  • Focus strictly on assigned tasks within your area of authority.
  • Maintain flexibility and be ready to rotate or stand down as directed.
