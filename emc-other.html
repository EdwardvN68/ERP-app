Other Members of the EMC – Emergency Response Role

Activation

This page provides role awareness for Other Members of the EMC. Activation and mobilisation are governed by the ERP and EMT direction.

Core Responsibilities

Other EMC members support the EMT/EMC function as directed by the Emergency Management Chairman and senior EMT leadership. Responsibilities will depend on the emergency, the EMC structure in use, and the tasking assigned during briefings.
